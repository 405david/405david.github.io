Our Team

Brain-Life is powered by a multi-disciplinary research group of experts in Brain-Computer Interface (BCI), Artificial Intelligence (AI), Internet of Things (IoT), Blockchain, and data privacy. Based in the UK with a dedicated research team in Vietnam, the Brain-Life team comprises experienced researchers from leading institutions such as International University - Vietnam National University and Thu Dau Mot University, leading research work packages (WPs) and mentoring young talents. Additionally, two Medical Doctors serve as hardware and results experts, ensuring the quality of our devices and outcomes. We have an advisory board of esteemed university leaders and experts (i.e., UCL, Vin University, Fulbright University Vietnam, and University of Medicine & Pharmacy HCMC) that provide invaluable guidance and networks to Brain-Life.

Blog/Publications

Brain-Life's aims and objectives are bolstered by the theoretical and practical outcomes of our research group at IU, in collaboration with esteemed institutions globally. We have successfully engineered affordable BCI devices, with hardware prototypes capable of real-time operation for EEG signal extraction. Our research group has published extensively on this topic. It has access to international R&D resources and laboratories for testing case studies in diverse user groups. To stay updated with our latest research findings, insights, and industry developments, explore our blog and publications section.
